Portrait · Autobiography

A quiet life, paid attention to.

On dance, running, psychology, and the slow practice of living with the lights on.

Zhanna, Netishyn · age 21

My name is Zhanna. I'm twenty-one, and I'm from Netishyn, a small town in Ukraine. I'm a creative and sensitive person who pays attention to details — someone who would rather feel, understand, and experience a thing deeply than drift through it on autopilot.

I love dance. For me, it's more than movement. It's a way of self-expression, a language of the body and emotions. Through dance, I can say things that are hard to put into words.

"I'm drawn to language and meaning — the way words carry weight, and texts carry ideas behind them."

Running and sports are an important part of my life. They help me build discipline, endurance, and inner balance. I care about my body, and I enjoy finding harmony between activity and recovery — the rhythm of effort and rest.

I'm deeply interested in people, psychology, relationships, and complex life choices — topics that deserve depth rather than surface-level conversations. I value honesty, tenderness, and genuine connection in communication, creativity, and in the ordinary moments of every day.

This website is my personal space — a reflection of my journey, my thoughts, and the things I make and feel.
