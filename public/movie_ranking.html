Essay · 01 · On Cinema

The best movies, according to me.

Three films. Not necessarily the "greatest" — just the ones that stayed. The ones I keep returning to, the way one returns to an old letter.

Hacksaw Ridge 2016

Ostensibly a war film — but really a film about freedom and faith, and the quiet courage of holding a line no one asked you to hold.

The Notebook 2004

A love story that refuses to let go — tender, stubborn, and honest about what it means to remember someone until the very end.

Attack on Titan 2013–2023

The best anime I know. Beneath the spectacle, a study of human nature — of how ordinary people become something else entirely when the walls come down.
